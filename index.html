Blog Posts

The World Wide Web

Tim Berners-Lee is a computer scientist that invented the World-Wide Web in 1993. The World-Wide Web was invented to be a pool of human knowledge where people could share their ideas and knowledge to other collaborators.

History of Web Robots

A web robot is also known as internet bot or bot. A bot is a software application whose purpose is to perform tasks on the internet. The tasks they perform are simple and repetitive and they perform the task at a much faster rate than a human.

What are browsers?

The word browse means to scan or skim. On a computer browse would mean scanning through the internet. A browser is a software application that allows users to surf through the World Wide Web a browser basically extracts information from the World Wide Web.
